How the DM concierge runs

Message in. Booking out. Five steps.

Each step runs on its own. If anything looks off, the message is flagged so you can step in.

1
Message in
Client sends a DM on Instagram, Facebook, or messages the WhatsApp widget on your site. The system picks it up immediately.
2
Understand
AI reads the message and decides: pricing question, booking, general chat, or something to flag for you.
3
Reply in your tone
Generates a reply trained on your past messages and the treatment knowledge from your website.
4
Route
Sends the right link, service page or booking, when the client is ready to act.
5
Capture
Logs the lead, name, treatment requested, contact, and source, straight into a Google Sheet.
